    Undergraduate Programs Eligibility

    Requirements for Bachelor's degree programs

    General Eligibility

    Educational Qualification

    10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board with minimum 50% aggregate marks (45% for SC/ST/OBC)

    Age Limit

    Minimum 17 years and maximum 25 years as on July 1st of the admission year

    Subject Requirements

    Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics for Engineering programs; Any stream for Arts/Commerce programs

    Entrance Examination

    Valid JEE Main score for Engineering programs; Merit-based for other programs

    Program-Specific Requirements

    B.Tech Programs

    Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics with minimum 60% aggregate

    BBA Programs

    Any stream with Mathematics/Business Mathematics

    B.Com Programs

    Commerce stream preferred, but any stream accepted

    BA Programs

    Any stream with good communication skills

    Postgraduate Programs Eligibility

    Requirements for Master's degree programs

    General Eligibility

    Educational Qualification

    Bachelor's degree in relevant field with minimum 55% aggregate marks (50% for SC/ST/OBC)

    Age Limit

    No upper age limit for most programs

    Work Experience

    Preferred for MBA programs (minimum 2 years)

    Entrance Examination

    GATE for M.Tech; CAT/MAT for MBA; Merit-based for other programs

    Program-Specific Requirements

    M.Tech Programs

    B.Tech/B.E. in relevant engineering discipline

    MBA Programs

    Any Bachelor's degree with work experience preferred

    M.Com Programs

    B.Com or equivalent degree in commerce

    MA Programs

    BA in relevant subject or equivalent

    Special Categories & Reservations

    Additional information for reserved categories and special cases

    SC/ST/OBC
    5% Relaxation

    5% relaxation in minimum percentage requirement for SC/ST/OBC candidates

    PwD Candidates
    5% Relaxation

    5% relaxation in minimum percentage requirement for Persons with Disabilities

    Women Candidates
    Special Consideration

    Special consideration and support for women candidates in all programs

    Important Notes

    Please read these important points before applying

    Document Verification

    All submitted documents will be verified. False information may lead to cancellation of admission.

    Provisional Admission

    Admission is provisional until all original documents are submitted and verified.

    Merit List

    Final selection will be based on merit, entrance exam scores, and availability of seats.
