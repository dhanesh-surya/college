    Admission Requirements

    Essential documents and eligibility criteria for admission

    Required Documents
    Academic Certificates

    10th and 12th mark sheets and certificates

    Identity Proof

    Aadhar Card, Passport, or Driving License

    Photographs

    Recent passport-size photographs (6 copies)

    Category Certificate

    SC/ST/OBC certificate (if applicable)

    Migration Certificate

    For students from other boards/universities

    Eligibility Criteria

    Minimum Percentage

    50% aggregate in qualifying examination (45% for reserved categories)

    Age Limit

    As per university norms (generally 17-25 years)

    Subject Requirements

    Specific subjects as per course requirements

    Entrance Exam

    Valid scores if required for specific programs

    Medical Fitness

    Medical certificate for certain courses
